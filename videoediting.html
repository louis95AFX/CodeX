The Art of Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video shots to create a new work. It's both a technical and creative process that transforms raw footage into a compelling story.

Key Concepts:

  • Non-linear editing (NLE): Modern digital editing workflow
  • Timeline: Where you arrange your clips
  • Cutting: The basic building block of editing
  • Continuity: Maintaining consistency between shots
  • Pacing: The rhythm of your edit
  • J-cut & L-cut: Audio leading into or out of a scene
