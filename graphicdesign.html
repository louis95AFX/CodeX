What is Graphic Design?

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It communicates messages through typography, imagery, color, and form to inform, persuade, and engage audiences.

Key Concepts:

  • Visual Communication: Conveying ideas through visuals
  • Problem Solving: Addressing communication challenges
  • Aesthetics: Creating visually appealing solutions
  • Functionality: Designs must serve their purpose
  • User Experience: Considering how people interact with designs
